THE LAND OF DESTINY...

Welcome to LOAM MORATH, a world trapped in an endless cycle known as the Weaves. Each Weave begins with the Mists swallowing the land, followed by the rise of the prophesized Light -- someone chosen to save the world. But every Light has failed, and the Mists always return.

This age, the Fourth Weave, is different. The Light is not a god, but a fallen human -- THE REPENTANT -- who may finally break the cycle. As gods rage war on fate itself, the last cities stand against the creeping dark, and an ancient force stirs once more...
